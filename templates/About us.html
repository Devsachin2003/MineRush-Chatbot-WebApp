Our chatbot is designed to be your reliable companion, offering timely responses and expert guidance to help you navigate the complexities of the industry.


Mission and Vision


We envision a future where accessing regulatory information is effortless, helping stakeholders stay compliant and informed. Our AI-powered chatbot is a step towards this future, enhancing efficiency and providing reliable support around the clock.Moreover, our commitment to user-centric design ensures that the chatbot is intuitive and accessible, providing a user-friendly interface that requires no specialized training. We are dedicated to pioneering solutions that bridge the gap between technology and user needs, fostering a more informed, compliant, and efficient industry environment.


What We Do


Our chatbot utilizes cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Natural Language Processing (NLP) technologies to understand and respond to customer inquiries. By imitating human conversation, it offers an intuitive and efficient way for users to access crucial information.


Comprehensive Coverage


In addition to mining regulations, our chatbot is equipped to handle queries related to land laws, such as the Coal Bearing Areas (Acquisition and Development) Act (CBA), Land Acquisition (LA) laws, and Rehabilitation and Resettlement (RandR) policies. This makes it a versatile tool for developing a Robust Management Information System.
